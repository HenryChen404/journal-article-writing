# Story design SOP

This is the shared AI–human operating sheet. The model pre-populates it and the user approves the editorial gates.

| Stage | AI responsibility | Human responsibility | Required artifact | Gate | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Assignment | infer and prefill brief | confirm reader, form, length, output | editorial-brief.md | G0 | pending |
| Story models | propose 2–3 evidence-seeking models | choose maturity and human center | candidate models | G1 | pending |
| Hypothesis | draft, narrow, define disproof | approve hypothesis and scope | story-hypothesis.md | G2 | pending |
| Research | search, interview plan, annotate OKF records | provide access, contacts, judgment | sources/ claims/ materials/ | — | pending |
| Architecture | group by reader questions and jobs | approve dominant line and directory | article-blueprint.md + sections/ | G3 | pending |
| Section movement | assign expansion tools | accept or override key sections | SEC cards | G4 | pending |
| Drafting | write from approved cards and materials | review meaning and emphasis | drafts/draft.md | — | pending |
| Voice | analyze sample and propose cleanup | choose voice and intensity | voice profile | G5 | pending |
| Verification | build ledger, flag gaps and fairness | decide publication risk | audits/ | G6 | pending |
| Revision | substance → structure → prose | approve final | final draft | — | pending |
